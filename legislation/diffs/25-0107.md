CITY OF BALTIMORE
COUNCIL BILL 25-0107
(First Reader)

Introduced by: The Council President
At the request of: The Administration (Department of Finance)
Introduced and read first time: October 20, 2025
Assigned to: Budget and Appropriations Committee
REFERRED TO THE FOLLOWING AGENCIES: City Solicitor, Department of Finance

A BILL ENTITLED
1 AN ORDINANCE concerning
2  Supplementary General Fund Operating Appropriation –
3 Department of Law – $81,393
4 FOR the purpose of providing a Supplementary General Fund Operating Appropriation in the
5 amount of $81,393 to the Department of Law – Service 862 (Transactions), to provide
6 funding to balance the Department of Law’s Fiscal Year 2025 budget to cover a deficit
7 caused by contractual spending; and providing for a special effective date.
8 BY authority of
9 Article VI - Board of Estimates
10 Section 8(b)(2)(iii) and (c)
11 Baltimore City Charter
12 (1996 Edition)
13 Recitals
14 The revenue appropriated by this Ordinance represents $81,393 in excess of the revenue
15 relied on by the Board of Estimates in determining the tax levy required to balance the budget for
16 Fiscal Year 2025.
17 This additional revenue could not have been reasonably anticipated when the Ordinance of
18 Estimates for Fiscal Year 2025 was formulated.
19 This appropriation is made necessary by a material change in circumstances since the
20 Ordinance of Estimates for Fiscal Year 2025 was formulated or is for a new program that could
21 not have been reasonably anticipated when that Ordinance of Estimates was formulated.
22 On October 15, 2025, the Board of Estimates recommended this appropriation to the City
23 Council.

Council Bill 25-0107
1 SECTION 1.  BE IT ORDAINED BY THE MAYOR AND CITY COUNCIL OF BALTIMORE, That
2 $81,393 shall be made available to the Department of Law – Service 862 (Transactions) as a
3 Supplementary General Fund Operating Appropriation for Fiscal Year 2025, to provide funding
4 to balance the Department of Law’s Fiscal Year 2025 budget to cover a deficit caused by
5 contractual spending.  The source of revenue for this appropriation is General Fund assigned
6 Fund Balance in excess of the amount from this source that was relied on by the Board of
7 Estimates in determining the tax levy required to balance the budget for Fiscal Year 2025.
8 SECTION 2.  AND BE IT FURTHER ORDAINED, That this Ordinance takes effect on the date it is
9 enacted.
