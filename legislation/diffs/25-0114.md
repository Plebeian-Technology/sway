13 “SMOKE SHOP” MEANS A RETAIL ESTABLISHMENT THAT:
14 (1) IS PRIMARILY ENGAGED IN THE RETAIL SALE OR ON-SITE CONSUMPTION OF
15 TOBACCO PRODUCTS, TOBACCO SMOKING ACCESSORIES, ANY DEVICE OR
16 PARAPHERNALIA THAT CAN BE USED TO DELIVER NICOTINE OR OTHER
17 SUBSTANCES, INCLUDING CANNABIS, TO THE INDIVIDUAL INHALING FROM THE
18 DEVICE, OR ANY CARTRIDGE, COMPONENT, OR ACCESSORY OF THE DEVICE; AND
19 (2) OPERATES UNDER 1 OR MORE OF THE FOLLOWING LICENSES ESTABLISHED BY THE
20 STATE BUSINESS REGULATION ARTICLE:
21 (I) OTHER TOBACCO PRODUCTS (OTP) RETAILERS, AS ESTABLISHED BY
22 § 16.5-201 {“LICENSES REQUIRED RELATING TO TOBACCO PRODUCTS”};
23 (II) ELECTRONIC SMOKING DEVICES RETAILER, AS ESTABLISHED BY § 16.7-201
24 {“LICENSE REQUIRED”};
25 (III) VAPE SHOP VENDOR, AS ESTABLISHED BY § 16.7-201 {“LICENSE
26  REQUIRED”}; OR
27 (IV) A CIGARETTE BUSINESS LICENSE, AS ESTABLISHED BY TITLE 16
28 {“CIGARETTES”}; AND
29 (3) DEVOTES 10% OR MORE OF ITS GROSS RETAIL FLOOR AREA TO THE COMBINED
30 TOTAL OF:
31 (I) OTHER TOBACCO PRODUCTS, AS DEFINED BY § 16.7-101(C) OF THE STATE
32 BUSINESS REGULATION ARTICLE; AND
