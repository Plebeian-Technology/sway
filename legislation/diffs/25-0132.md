26 SECTION 2.  AND BE IT FURTHER ORDAINED, That the proceedings for the condemnation and
27 closing of of North Vincent Street, Forney Street, and a 2 foot alley bounded by West Lexington
28 Street, North Gilmor Street, West Fayette Street, and North Mount Street and the rights of all
29 interested parties shall be regulated by and in accordance with all applicable provisions of state
30 and local law and with all applicable rules and regulations adopted by the Director of
31 Transportation and filed with the Department of Legislative Reference.
32 SECTION 3.  AND BE IT FURTHER ORDAINED, That after the closing under this Ordinance, all
33 subsurface structures and appurtenances now owned by the Mayor and City Council of Baltimore
34 continue to be the property of the Mayor and City Council, in fee simple, until their use has been
35 abandoned by the Mayor and City Council.  If any person wants to remove, alter, or interfere
36 with them, that person must first obtain permission from the Mayor and City Council and, in the
37 application for this permission, must agree to pay all costs and expenses, of every kind, arising
38 out of the removal, alteration, or interference.

17 or pay compensation to the owner of the property.

19 enacted.
