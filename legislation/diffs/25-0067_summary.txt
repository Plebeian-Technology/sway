Residency requirements – City officials generally. 16 (a) Definitions. 17 (1) In general. 18 In this section, the following terms have the meanings indicated. 19 (2) ACTING CAPACITY. 20 “ACTING CAPACITY” MEANS A SITUATION IN WHICH AN EMPLOYEE TEMPORARILY 21 PERFORMS A JOB OR FUNCTION OTHER THAN THE JOB OR FUNCTION NORMALLY 22 ASSIGNED TO THAT EMPLOYEE.
