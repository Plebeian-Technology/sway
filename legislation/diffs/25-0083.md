Re: City Council Bill 25-0083 – RPP Area 30 – Exception – 15 E West Street

Dear President and City Council Members:

The Law Department has reviewed City Council Bill 25-0083 for form and legal
sufficiency. The bill would amend the Parking Management Plan for RPP Area 30 to add an
exception to the Plan’s general permit allotments for dwelling units . The exception pertains to a
multi-family dwelling development at 15 E. West Street.

Once a parking management plan has been adopted by administrative regulation, it may be
modified by ordinance. City Code, Art. 31, § 10-19(a). Accordingly, the Law Department approves
the bill for form and legal sufficiency.
